Pumpkin Muffin
Ingredients
49.61 grams All-Purpose Flour; 
28.35 grams White Sugar; 
14.17 grams Dark Brown Sugar; 
28.35 grams Baking Soda; 
14.17 grams Salt; 
56.7 grams Cinnamon; 
7.09 grams Ground Cloves; 
7.09 grams Nutmeg; 
56.7 grams 
0.15 liters Can Pure Pumpkin Purée; 
0.005 liters Coconut Oil, Melted; 
28.35 grams Vanilla Extract; 
Temperature
188.65 celsius 
Instructions
1) Preheat the oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit, and place 12 paper liners into each well of your standard size muffin baking pan.
2) Measure out the flour, sugars, baking soda, salt and spices in a medium bowl and whisk together. Set aside.
3) In another bowl, whisk together the eggs, pumpkin purée, coconut oil and vanilla extract.
4) Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir together. Do not over mix, just stir until everything is
incorporated into the batter.
5)Use a large scoop (like an ice cream scoop) to evenly distribute the batter into each muffin well. They will be nearly
full.
6)Bake muffins for 20-22 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of muffin comes out clean.
