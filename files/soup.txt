Zucchini Soup
Ingredients:
&1 Cup Chicken Stock;
&8 Ounce Cream;
!1 Cup Zucchini, chopped;
!1 Medium Onion, chopped;
!2 teaspoon Salt;
!1 Tablespoon Paprika;
!1 Tablespoon Chili Powder;
Temperature:
@150 Fahrenheit;
Instructions:
Fry the Onions and Zucchini until translucent. Transfer to blender with chicken stalk. Blend until fully
emulsified. Add cream and blend until evenly distributed. Add to soup pot and add salt, paprika,
and chili powder. Heat until it reaches 150 degrees Fahrenheit.
